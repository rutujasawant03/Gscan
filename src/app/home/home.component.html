About G-Scan

About us


G-Scan allows shoppers to pick up an item they wish to buy, scan the barcode of the item with their phone and pay. they simply leave with the item they just bought No queuing; just Scan, Pay and Leave. The customers can buy the products either online by Click & Collect feature or offline by Scan & Go feature.

Scan&GO


This feature allows customers to pick up an item from the grocery store they wish to buy, scan the product barcode with their phone and pay for it on the website, after that they can simply leave with the item they just bought. No queuing; just Scan, Pay and Leave.

Click&GO


This feature allows customers to pick up an item from the grocery store they wish to buy, scan the product barcode with their phone and pay for it on the website, after that they can simply leave with the item they just bought. No queuing; just Scan, Pay and Leave.

EasyPay


We have provided two payment modes to the Customers online &offline. In online mode, the customers can pay the bill by using the UPI appsand in offline mode, the customer can pay the bill by using the Pay in Shop option. Where the customer can directly pay the bill to the shopkeeper if there is any problem in the online mode.
